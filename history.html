Since its inception, this team has received numerous achievements. Back in 2017, they participated in "ISIE-HYBRID VEHICLE CHALLENGE 2016-2017", where they have successfully achieved two prizes in the area of ISIE Future HVC. Team Swapnojan competed in "Formula Bharat 2021" in 2021, and this time they won two trophies for the 'Best formula student debut', which is a remarkable note as an automotive team. In this competition, they also won the award for 'Innovative Use of Material' for successfully implementing 'Jute' as a material in their car body. 

Team Swapnojan, a well-known Formula Student from AUST that was founded in 2015. This is a Formula Student team consisting of over 25 undergraduate students from the AUST.This team is currently working on the next Formula student competition for this year, as well as many more in the coming years.

We thank you all for your continued support and enthusiasm.
